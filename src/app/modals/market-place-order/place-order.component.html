Place order

Warning: you are about to bid on a real listing on development version of Particl Marketplace. You will need to spend real PART coins when Seller accepts your bid afterwards! Bugs may occur & your payment might get lost – continue on your own risk!

Are you sure you want to place this bid?

Before paying for this order, your bid needs to be accepted by the Seller. After it is accepted, you can pay for the order in Buy > Orders.

Accept Bid

Warning: you are about to accept a bid and lock real PART coins in escrow on development version of Particl Marketplace. Bugs may occur & your payment might get lost – continue on your own risk! You also must ship the item you're selling when Buyer pays for it!

Are you sure you want to accept this bid?

Before the payment can be made, you need to accept the bid made by the Buyer.

Reject Bid

Are you sure you want to reject this bid?

Before the payment can be made, you need to accept the bid made by the Buyer.
By rejecting this bid, the Buyer will not be able to purchase your item.

Confirm order delivery

Warning: only confirm the delivery if you really received the item in real life. Real PART coins will be unlocked from escrow after this confirmation. If you confirm delivery and didn't receive the item, you will lose money!

Are you sure your received your order?

Confirming successful delivery will finalize the order and release your funds locked in escrow.
